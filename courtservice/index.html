Court Services
NationwideLegal | Legal Services Company |

We offer a variety of on-demand court services to help you with your legal needs in any court in the United States. These services include counter filing, delivery of copies to the court, research of case documents, and recording of documents. Our online tracking system allows you to receive updates and confirmations about the progress and completion of your requests via email. You will also have access to these documents within the job details for a period of 7 years.
 

  • Counter Filing: When required, we will physically file your documents at any court in the United States within your deadline.
     

  • Court Research: We will retrieve filed documents from any court in the United States within your required deadline.
     

  • Courtesy Copy: We will deliver copies of your documents directly to the judge in any court in the United States.
     

  • Advance Fees: We will pay the court filing fees on your behalf in any court in the United States.
