Investigations
NationwideLegal | Legal Services Company |

Our team is dedicated to providing professional, confidential, and reliable investigation services to a wide range of clients. Whether you are a business owner looking to protect your company's interests, an individual seeking answers to a personal matter, or a lawyer seeking assistance with a case, we have the expertise and resources to help. Our services include surveillance, background checks, skip traces, asset searches, cyber crime investigations and more. We take pride in our attention to detail, discretion, and commitment to getting results.

  • Skip Trace: We use advanced search techniques and database resources to locate individuals who have gone missing or are difficult to find. We can help you locate missing persons, debtors, witnesses, or other individuals for a variety of reasons.
     

  • Asset Search: We can locate and identify assets that may be hidden or undeclared, including financial assets, property, vehicles, and other valuable items for a variety of purposes.
     

  • Surveillance: Detailed and accurate observations of individuals or locations. We use state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to gather evidence, monitor activities, and report our findings in a clear and concise manner.
     

  • Background Checks: We provide thorough and reliable information on individuals or businesses. We can verify employment history, education, criminal records, and other relevant details to help you make informed decisions.
