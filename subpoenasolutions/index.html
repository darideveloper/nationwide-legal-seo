Subpoena Services
NationwideLegal | Legal Services Company |

We offer comprehensive subpoena services to assist law firms in the preparation and execution of subpoenas. Our highly secure online document repository allows for increased efficiency and represents a source of competitive advantage, particularly in the data-intensive legal field. Our team of experienced professionals can handle all aspects of the subpoena process, including:
 

  • Deposition officer duties: We professionally maintain your subpoenaed records and follow up with facilities to ensure we meet your deposition dates.
     

  • Medical and Business Records Production: We can assist with the production of medical and business records by subpoena or authorization.
     

  • Bundled Pricing: We offer bundled pricing for all of our subpoena services, including preparation, service, production, and retention.
     

  • Domestication Services for Out-of-State Subpoenas: We can handle the process of domestication for out-of-state subpoenas, ensuring that they are properly served and recognized in the jurisdiction where they are being enforced.
